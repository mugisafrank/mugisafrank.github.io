Blur

Blur is an arcade-style racing video game for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 3, and Xbox 360. It was developed by Bizarre Creations and published by Activision. It features over 50 licensed cars, including custom models, 35 tracks in 14 real-world-inspired locations, visual damage modeling, and arcade-style car physics.
