Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game that takes place in the sprawling city of Los Santos and its surrounding areas. Developed by Rockstar North, this critically acclaimed game offers a massive and detailed open world for players to explore.


Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, GTA V follows the lives of three criminals: Michael De Santa, Franklin Clinton, and Trevor Philips.
Players can switch between these characters at any time, each bringing their unique skills and personality to the storyline.
The narrative weaves through heists, crime, and the pursuit of the American Dream.
